About

NorthernCoalition was created in November 2016 and is made up mainly of students from the different universities of the province of Québec, Canada. The rationale behind the team is to combine the strength of all our the members in order to stand out more internationally.

Teams and universities

  • CFI-UL — Université Laval
  • DCIETS — École technologie supérieure
  • JDIS — Université de Sherbrooke
  • AGEEI — Université du Québec à Montréal
  • PolyHx — École Polytechnique de Montréal
